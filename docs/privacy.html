Neptune — Privacy Policy

Effective 2026-07-11 · Neptune is a product of Ayers Electronics (authored by Ayers Software Repair).

Neptune is self-hosted software that runs on your own hardware. We do not operate a cloud service, we do not have user accounts, and we collect nothing about you or your media.

What we collect

Nothing. The Neptune apps and server contain no analytics, no telemetry, no advertising or tracking SDKs, and no crash-reporting that phones home. There is no Neptune account and no login to any Neptune-operated service, because no such service exists. Your library, your viewing, your channels, and your settings stay on the machines you run Neptune on.

What the app connects to

The Neptune client app connects to one thing you choose: your own Neptune server, at the address you enter. It does not send data anywhere else. The Neptune server, in turn, only contacts services you configure or explicitly ask for:

  • Your own media files — read locally, never modified, never uploaded.
  • Metadata providers (optional) — if you supply your own TMDb API key, the server requests artwork and descriptions from TMDb for titles you already own. No personal data is sent; this is off unless you enable it.
  • Usenet servers & indexers (optional) — if you configure the acquisition feature, the server connects to the news servers and indexers you provide, using your credentials, which are stored locally on your server. Neptune never sees them. There is no BitTorrent support of any kind.
  • Optional components you approve — bundled media tooling ships with the app; the optional on-device AI assistant downloads its model only after you explicitly enable it, and then runs entirely locally with no cloud calls.

Data storage

All Neptune data — catalog, channels, watch progress, settings, credentials — is stored in a local database on the server you run. It is never transmitted to us. Deleting Neptune deletes its data.

Children's privacy

Neptune does not collect personal information from anyone, including children. It is a media player for content you already own and provide.

Third parties

We do not sell, share, or transfer any information to third parties, because we do not collect any. Optional connections listed above go to services you control, under your own accounts.
